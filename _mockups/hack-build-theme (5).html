WHO I AM

Moheeb Zara

Hardware hacker, new media artist, and dropout. I make things that blink, move, and occasionally require a fire extinguisher nearby.

I work at the intersection of embedded systems, creative coding, and projection mapping. I write tutorials, build tools, and host workshops because expensive proprietary stuff annoys me. If I can make it cheaper and open source, I will.

Former board member at HeatSync Labs — one of the longest-running hackerspaces in the Southwest. Co-founder of South West Maker Festival. I believe in do-ocracy and showing up.

"This all started because I was annoyed. The options out there are expensive and sparse. We live in the future — let's update things to reflect that." — Me, on most projects

@virgilvox

IoT, robots, javascripts, art, Star Trek. Mesa, AZ.
